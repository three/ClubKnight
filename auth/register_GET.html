Before you register

Who can register?

Students of Rutgers University, 18 years of age or older.

What do I need to register

To register you need your Rutgers netId. An email will be sent to netid@scarletmail.rutgers.edu with a confirmation code. Or that's what's supposed to happend once we're done. In the game you will be identified by your netId.

Am I anonymous?

Nobody is anonymous when using Club RU. Each student is identified by their netID.

Can I be banned?

Short of legal concerns (you must obey the law), we do not ban people.

Do you value your user's privacy?

The ClubRU team recognizes the importance of keeping users informed what data we collect and how we use it. For this reason, we have included many questions on this page with the purpose of answering those questions. This does NOT necessarily mean we are conservative or careful about the data we do collect, so please read the ENTIRE page and email us if you have additional questions (eric AT threedot14.com).

What do you log?

The information we log from the server changes depending on how much we have the capacity to log and what we feel we must log to improve ClubRU. We always log all chat interactions. If you are a user of ClubRU it is safest to assume all your online interactions will be logged.

How long do you store logs?

We currently do not have routines to regularly delete log files. Log files may be deleted when space becomes low. If you are a user of ClubRU it is safest to assume log files will be stored forever.

Do you sell any data you collect?

ClubRU will NEVER sell any data we collect.

Do you cooperate with police?

We do not have an official policy for cooperating with police. We will always cooperate with the police if the law requires we do so. This may include providing access logs, chat logs, or any other data collected and stored by our server. Additionally, there have been cases where police have seized control of servers, giving them the ability to collect or ask for additional information. If you are a user of ClubRU it is safest to assume we will fully cooperate with the police.

Is ClubRU open source?

The source for ClubRU is publicly available on our Github Repo, however we have not yet chosen a proper license to be considered "open source".

Are you accepting contributions?

Yes! Please follow the contributing guidelines on our Github repo.

How are passwords stored?

Your passwords are salted and hashed using argon2.

How do I change my password?

Come back to this page and enter your netId and new password. Once you confirm the password change via email, we will update our database to reflect it.

Are you affiliated with Rutgers?

Club RU is made by a team of Rutgers students but is NOT affiliated in any way with the University. We run independently of Rutgers University.

What else do I need to know?
